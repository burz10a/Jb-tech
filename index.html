JB-Tech

JB-Tech is a premium tech channel focused on real smartphone testing, flagship reviews and honest comparisons.

We don’t use marketing scripts — every phone is tested in real life conditions: camera, battery, performance and daily usage.

The channel is run by two creators who film, test and edit everything together, building a clean Apple-style identity.

Our main device is the Vivo X300 Pro, used for most recordings, photography tests and social content.

Main device: Vivo X300 Pro

More Than a Tech Channel

JB-Tech is not just about phones — it’s about showing how technology feels in real life. We focus on premium devices from brands like Vivo, Samsung and Huawei, testing them in everyday situations. Each video is designed with a clean, minimal Apple-inspired style. No fake hype, no scripted marketing — just real usage experience. The goal is to help viewers understand which phone actually fits their lifestyle, not just which one has the highest specs on paper.
